# Privacy Policy – Crazy Popping Catz

Crazy Popping Catz does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

The game does not require an account and does not request any personal information.

No analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies are used.

The game only stores gameplay information locally on the user's device (such as score), and this data is never transmitted externally.
